About Me

You deserve to know more about me since you've made it this far. I'm an Ottawa native who attended Immaculata High School for 4 years before Queen's University. During this time, I played hockey, baseball, soccer and golf for my respective club and high school teams. At Queen's, I still find time to play intramural hockey, soccer, and golf whenever possible.

I love all sports and watch them all.

Along with my love of sports, I also love the outdoors and fishing. I've been fishing since I was 7, and I usually start and end the day on the water during the summer.

In between all the sports and fishing, video games have always been a fun pastime of mine. Playing and then learning how to fix my constantly slowing laptop were forever hand in hand. Through this tinkering of software and hardware, I found a passion for computers, and that's how I decided on computer engineering. So, when learning how to fix computers on a deeper level became available, I pursued it as a career.
